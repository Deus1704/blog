The Very Essence of Coalignment At its core, coalignment is all about making sure our solar images match up as best they can. It uses a few different techniques, like template matching and solar rotation correction, to get this done. This is really important when we’re trying to accurately track and study what’s happening on a part of sun over time. We re-thought the entire process of what our new API be providing to the users as well as its structure.
